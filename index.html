About SR Builders

Welcome to SR Builders, a leading construction and property development company in Karimnagar . The firm has over thirty years of experience in the residential real estate sector. The company has more than 15 large residential projects of more than 500 apartments each, to the tune of about Rs. 7000 Crores and is actively expanding by adding several new projects. The company is proud of the reputation it has built for honesty and quality of construction. We have a “Customer First”, attitude and strongly believe in transparency in all that we do. All our projects have competitive pricing as we only build on company owned land and all our construction is in-house. Since our inception we have provided thousands of peoples with their dream homes . We hope we can do the same for you!
